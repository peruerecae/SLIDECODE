ARLECCHINO

(Peruere Snezhevna)

Appearance

Arlecchino is a fairly tall woman who has pale skin and eyes that has pure black irises and a red "x"-shaped pupil.
Her asymmetric long length snow white hair has an occasional black streak swept to the right side of her head.
She wears small black and white snowflake-pattern earrings.

Occupation

Fourth of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers, Manager of House of the Hearth

Vision

Pyro

Weapon

Polearm

Constellation

Ignis Purgatorius
